# Tenant as organization with role-based members

A Tenant is an organization with Members, linked Guilds, and Campaigns attached. Member Roles within a Tenant: `owner`, `admin`, `gm`. Campaigns belong to the Tenant (not the GM personally), so reassigning the GM is a Member Role change inside the Tenant rather than an ownership transfer. Cross-tenant transfer is out of scope for v1.0; an export-archive is a separate future feature.
